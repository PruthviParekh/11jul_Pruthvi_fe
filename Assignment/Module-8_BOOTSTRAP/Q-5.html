What is the difference between Bootstrap 4 and Bootstrap 5

Bootstrap 4

- It has 5 tier like xs, sm, md, lg, xl.

- It has limited colours.

- It has jquery and all related plugins.

- It supports both IE 10 and 11.

- It doesn't support Offcanvas component.

Bootstrap 5

- It has 6 tier like xs, sm, md, lg, xl, xxl.

- Extra colours and shades available to choose.

- Jquery is removed and switched to vanilla JS.

- It doesn't support IE 10 and 11.

- It supports Offcanvas component.
