Find circumference of Rectangle formula : C = 4 * a ?

- The formula C = 4a is not the correct formula for the circumference (perimeter) of a rectangle. The correct formula to find the perimeter P of a rectangle is:

- P = 2 * (length+width)

- Where the length and width are the dimensions of the rectangle.
